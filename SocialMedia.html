Social Media

Social Media is a ever growing industry, it is merging into all sectors of the world and is at the forefront of humans

Social Media is used in an array of ways, from marketting to publication to simple entertainment.

The way it is used depends on who is using it, for example if a company is using it, they'd typically use it for advertisement/marketting but if a simple content creator uses it, they would really use it for entertaining the audience.

Although, Social Media isn't just a medium for what is mentioned above, it is also used as a way of finding out news, typically news that isn't covered by mainstream media. With this form of media sharing, creates a whole different field of how people persieve the world, society and the major corporations running everything.

The difference between a mainstream company such as the BBC is the fact that Social Media has a sense of free will, in which anyone can use it, post on it and share their thoughts but a company would more or less block out content they do not agree to or would not voice certain subjecets that may stain their own reputation.

In the end, it all depends on who is controlling the Social Media account, because that is who controls the narrative.

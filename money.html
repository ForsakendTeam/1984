Money

Money is the most powerful weapon for anything in the media, power of money can bring anything to your fingertips.

Money is the central key for making things better or bad. Fair or unfair it's up to people's choice. Nowadays the media have so much power because of the power of money. Especially,social media gets so much money from all the companies,politicians,small businesses and general audience etc. In this way social media gives good and bad influence to the people to decide what to do. They run campaigns according to their needs,they run adverts on media to get what they want, this can lead to good or bad agencies.

For example, the media runs so many advertisements and bombard with different choices to select with and it's tempting us to buy their products or services,as well as collect our data. They are tied to us, targeting us based on our age, gender, household income, interests, and buying habits and we use our money to buy their product and service just from a simple video or image.

Money can change our life overnight with few simple adverts/campaigns on media, the media can make us in to anything like rich,poor,fear,unstable or stable lifestyle

It's all about how we use money wisely. That's why money is so powerful in terms of media.
